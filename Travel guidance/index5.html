Welcome to India Travel Guide

Discover the incredible diversity of India - from majestic mountains to pristine beaches, ancient temples to modern cities. Experience the rich culture, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality that makes India truly unforgettable.

Popular Destinations in India

1. Taj Mahal, Agra

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh

The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This white marble mausoleum was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Visit during sunrise or sunset for breathtaking views.

Best Time to Visit: October to March

How to Reach: Agra is well connected by train and road from Delhi (230 km away)

2. Jaipur - The Pink City

Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan

Jaipur is famous for its pink-colored buildings, magnificent forts, and vibrant culture. Visit the Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and explore the colorful bazaars. Don't miss the traditional Rajasthani cuisine and handicrafts.

Best Time to Visit: November to February

How to Reach: Well connected by air, rail, and road. 280 km from Delhi

3. Goa Beaches

Location: Goa

Goa is India's beach paradise with golden sands, swaying palm trees, and vibrant nightlife. Enjoy water sports, visit Portuguese churches, explore spice plantations, and savor fresh seafood. Famous beaches include Baga, Calangute, Anjuna, and Palolem.

Best Time to Visit: November to February

How to Reach: Dabolim Airport, connected by trains and buses from major cities

4. Kerala Backwaters

Location: Alleppey (Alappuzha), Kerala

Experience the serene beauty of Kerala's backwaters on a traditional houseboat. Cruise through tranquil canals, witness village life, enjoy authentic Kerala cuisine, and watch beautiful sunsets. Kerala is also known as "God's Own Country".

Best Time to Visit: September to March

How to Reach: Cochin Airport (85 km), well connected by rail and road

5. Varanasi - The Spiritual Capital

Location: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Varanasi is one of the oldest living cities in the world and the spiritual heart of India. Witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat, take a boat ride on the holy Ganges, and explore ancient temples and narrow lanes.

Best Time to Visit: October to March

How to Reach: Well connected by air, rail, and road from major cities

6. Ladakh - Land of High Passes

Location: Leh, Ladakh, Union Territory of Ladakh

Ladakh offers breathtaking mountain landscapes, crystal-clear lakes, ancient monasteries, and thrilling adventure activities. Visit Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, and experience the unique Tibetan Buddhist culture. Perfect for adventure enthusiasts.

Best Time to Visit: May to September

How to Reach: Leh Airport, or by road from Manali/Srinagar (roads open May-Oct)

7. Mysore Palace

Location: Mysore, Karnataka

The magnificent Mysore Palace is a stunning example of Indo-Saracenic architecture. Visit during the Dussehra festival when the palace is illuminated with thousands of lights. Also explore Chamundi Hills, St. Philomena's Church, and the famous Mysore silk sarees.

Best Time to Visit: October to February

How to Reach: Well connected by road and rail. 170 km from Bangalore

Travel Tips for India

Before You Go

  • Check visa requirements for your country
  • Get necessary vaccinations
  • Book accommodations in advance during peak season
  • Carry copies of important documents
  • Learn a few basic Hindi phrases

Packing Essentials

  • Comfortable walking shoes and sandals
  • Light cotton clothes (dress modestly when visiting religious places)
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, and hat
  • Insect repellent
  • Basic medicines and first aid kit
  • Power adapter (India uses 230V, Type C, D, and M plugs)

Safety and Health Tips

  • Drink only bottled or purified water
  • Be cautious with street food (choose busy, clean stalls)
  • Use registered taxis or ride-sharing apps
  • Keep valuables secure
  • Respect local customs and dress codes
  • Be prepared for crowds at popular tourist spots
